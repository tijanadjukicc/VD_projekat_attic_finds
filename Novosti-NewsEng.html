Painting workshop

The painting workshop organized by our art gallery is a unique opportunity for all art lovers to venture into the world of colors, textures and creativity. This inspiring workshop, which will be held in our beautifully decorated gallery space, will give participants the opportunity to learn new painting techniques and express their artistic vision.

At the head of this exceptional workshop are recognized painters and artists, who will share their rich knowledge and experience with the participants. Among our lecturers are Ana Petrović, a prominent painter of abstract art, who will lead a workshop on creative experimentation with colors and textures, and Marko Ivanović, a master of realism, who will focus on the technique of portrait painting. This diversity of lecturers allows students to delve into different styles of painting and explore their affinities.



The painting workshops will work on different topics and techniques, adapted to the level and interests of the participants. Participants will have the opportunity to learn the basics of composition, perspective, the use of color and shadows, as well as technical painting skills. Through practical exercises, demonstrations and individual support from lecturers, each participant will have the opportunity to develop their skills and express their artistic interpretation.

In addition, thematic discussions about art will be organized at the workshops, as well as visits to our rich art collection, which will further enrich the experience of the participants. Our gallery offers an inspiring ambience and a creative atmosphere that encourages artistic creativity.



A painting workshop in our art gallery is the perfect way to express creativity, explore new painting techniques and technologies, and connect with other art lovers. Regardless of experience and painting skills, everyone is welcome to join this inspiring workshop and explore their artistic side. Join us and let's create masterpieces on canvas together!

The workshops will take place every week from May 12, 2023. until 12.07.2023. according to the schedule below.
