Sunflower seeds

Chinese artist Ai Weiwei's installation consisted of as many as 100 million porcelain sunflower seeds and at the end of 2010 it was exhibited in the Turbine Hall gallery at Tate Modern. London's Tate gallery bought only a small part of the sculptor, i.e. eight million hand-painted seeds. As there are not enough seeds to be scattered on the ground of Turbine Hall as at the first exposure, they will be placed in a circle with a diameter of five meters and a height of about one and a half meters.
