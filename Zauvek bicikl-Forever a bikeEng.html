Forever a bike

Ai Weiwei, artistic superstar and recently probably the most popular name in the world of art scene created a spatial installation of 3,144 bicycles. This is another version of his Forever Bicycles project concept, which is impressive and truly monumental pays tribute to the most important means of transportation today - the bicycle. The surreal figure of 3,144 bicycles is arranged in a massive spatial installation, interconnected so to form a visually striking structure, the dynamics and impressiveness of which is further enhanced by the illumination of the installation itself. The project was installed in Nathan Phillips Square in Toronto, as part of the Scotiabank Nuit Blanche project. His first bicycle installation consisted of a thousand bicycles and was placed in an assembly his solo exhibitions in Taipei in 2011.
