Persistence of memory

In the original, La persistence de la memoria, and in the translation Persistence of memory, is a painting made in oil on canvas technique. Its dimensions are only 24 cm x 33 cm, and it is signed by probably the most important representative of surrealist art. movement - Salvador Dali. He painted Persistence of Memory in 1931, when he was 28 years old. It was shown for the first time in the Julien Levy Gallery in 1932, and since 1934 it has been in the MoMA museum in New York. Besides being one of Dali's most famous paintings, it is also one of his most significant surrealist works of art.
